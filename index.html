Info About Me

My name is Muhammad Danial Bin Yusri and I am a 20 years old individual currently studying Diploma in New Media Communication and Contentpreneurship.  My main cores is digital content creation, web development,  animation and game creation.  Since i was young, i am passionate on becoming a content creator. I mostly create a video related contents. I first started delving making contents and videos when i was 11 years old. My first content that i had made is a video animation using GoAnimate and uploaded in Youtube. After that, i have stopped for awhile until furthing my study in UiTM. During my studies in university, i have learned on how to make contents using various softwares such as Adobe Premiere and Adobe Photoshop. With the skills that i have learned throughout my studies, i am confident that i can be of help and learn more about the creative industry and how it works. I am looking forward to be a part of your company's team.        

Expertise and Experience

During my studies, i have learned diverse skillset and softwares. I am most proficient using the software Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe Photoshop. For our course, we mostly do a lot of video editing and content creating on social media. Starting first semester. i had dived into the video editing skills and learned on how to use the softwares. While there is room for improvement, i can say that i am more comfortable and confident in using Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe Photoshop. I have also had the experience creating my own company and brand and promoting it on social media such as Instagram, Tiktok and Facebook. Me and my team had created two social media accounts for our brand which is SeaShirt, Lucent Beauty and byyhzeraabdhalim. We had to manage and promote by creating postings and boosting our posts. We also had to design the logo, merchandise and the packaging. I also had the experience creating a website from template and from scratch using Adobe Dreamweaver. With that, i am knowledgeable in coding and can understand the functions. From semerster 2 until semester 4 me and my team had to create a functional game and publish it for our annual event GameInc22.

Academic History

I went to SK Seksyen 7 for about two month when i was 7 years old and moved overseas following my mother doing her PhD in England, Preston city. I was schooled near to where my house is which is Deepdale Community Primary School for 4 and a half years and then moved back to Malaysia because my mother had finished her PhD. Afterwards, i was schooled back to my old school which is SK Seksyen 7 to finish my primary school and take UPSR. Following my results, i continue to go to my high school, SMK Jalan Reko, Kajang. After SPM, i applied for UiTM Lendu, Alor Gajah for my continuation studies which is Diploma in New Media Communication and Contentpreneurship.
